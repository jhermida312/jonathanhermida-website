Presence & Emotional Mastery

How I Work

The Pillars of My Practice

My work holds two things at once: enough depth to reach what's real, and enough ground to bring it into your life.

01

Presence Over Performance

True leadership emerges from being, not doing. I help leaders develop the capacity to hold complexity, ambiguity, and high stakes from a grounded, spacious interior.

02

Inside-Out Development

Lasting change begins within. Rather than overlaying new behaviors on old patterns, we work at the level of identity, belief, and embodied awareness.

03

Wholeness as Strategy

Your relationships, health, and inner life are not separate from your leadership; they are the foundation of it. Integration is the ultimate performance edge.

The Process

What to Expect

A coaching engagement with me follows a clear arc, from deep listening to sustained transformation.

1

Discovery Conversation

We begin with a complimentary conversation to explore your context, your goals, and whether this partnership is the right fit.

2

Intake & Assessment

A structured exploration of where you are, what's working, what isn't, and what you most want to create in this next chapter.

3

Deep Coaching Sessions

Regular 1:1 sessions, typically bi-weekly, that combine insight, inquiry, somatic awareness, and accountability into real shifts.

4

Integration & Evolution

Between sessions you'll apply, integrate, and notice. Over time, the changes compound across your leadership, your relationships, and how you experience yourself.
